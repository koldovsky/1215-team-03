Itinerary

Salerno

On the first day, our task is to get to the marina get on board and give the trip a head up. Skippers provide the yachting briefing, you also can buy food and drinks and relax, preparing for start.

Positano

We go out to sea. Our goal is Positano, a very cozy, beautiful, truly Neapolitan town. We will stop in a small city bay, go ashore and go for a walk. When we get tired - we will visit one of the local home restaurants, where the owner will offer us cold wine and fresh seafood.

Pompeii

We transfer the past of Italy, Pompeii. During the eruption of Vesuvius, the city was buried under a multi-meter layer of ash in 79 BC, and today there is a huge open-air museum, the legacy of UNESCO. In the afternoon, it is possible to bathe offshore and have some rest on the yachts

Ischia Island

Another aristocratic and fascinating place. The main attraction of the island is the majestic castle, guarding the Bay of Naples from uninvited guests. We climb the hill and wander along its walls. Also, there are thermal springs which are used by local SPA-centers. You can visit them at your wish.

Procida, Sorrento

Literally half an hour in the morning to the island of Procida- and we are having breakfast in its beautiful bay. Then we raise the anchor and go to Sorrento - another beautiful, wonderful, real Neapolitan town. We spend the evening walking along Sorrento: here we will see the church of San Francesco of the 18th century and the Basilica of St. Antonio.

Amalfi

On the way to Amalfi we will stop at the Grotta di Smeraldo, a cave with real stalactites and stalagmites. It is equipped for tourists, everything is beautifully highlighted. Amalfi literally built in the cliff. The history in Amalfi is mixed with modernity: on the streets along which the Roman emperors walked, today there are youth bars and restaurants. It will be noisy, fun and romantic. After a night in Amalfi, we set back to Salerno not forgetting to take photos and swimming in the sea.
